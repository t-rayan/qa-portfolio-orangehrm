# BUG_002 — Date of Birth accepts today's date with no validation error

**Module:** My Info → Personal Details
**Severity:** Medium
**Priority:** Low
**Status:** Open
**Found by:** Narayan Thapa
**Date:** 25/03/2026

---

## Environment
- Browser: Chrome Version 146.0.7680.80 (Official Build) (arm64)
- OS: macOS Tahoe 26.3.1
- URL: https://opensource-demo.orangehrmlive.com
- Found during: Exploratory Testing

---

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Login with Username: Admin / Password: admin123
2. Click My Info in the left sidebar
3. Click Personal Details
4. Locate the Date of Birth field
5. Set the date to today's date 25/03/2026<img width="1464" height="882" alt="BUG_002" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d1cdce43-13ee-4fce-ad23-4757eac55046" />

6. Click Save

---

## Expected Result
Validation error shown. Today's date should not be accepted
as a valid date of birth — a person cannot be 0 days old.
The app should require a minimum age or at least reject
the current date.

---

## Actual Result
Record saved successfully. No validation error shown.
Today's date is accepted as a valid date of birth.

---

## Additional Testing
- Future dates (year 2026 onwards) are correctly blocked
  by the date picker — the calendar does not allow
  selecting future dates
- This means the app has partial validation — future dates
  are blocked but today's date and very recent dates
  are incorrectly accepted

## Severity Justification
Rated Medium because:
- Invalid data can be saved to the database
- A person with today's date as DOB would have age = 0
  which could cause calculation errors elsewhere in the
  system (e.g. age-based payroll or leave calculations)
- No data loss or security risk but data integrity
  is compromised

## Suggested Fix
Add server-side validation to reject any date of birth
that is today's date or within the last 15 years
(minimum working age) depending on business rules.
